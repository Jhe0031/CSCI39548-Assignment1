Charities

All it takes is a click of a button and a kind heart.

Before proceeding to donate, please refer to our donate page for details on what can and cannot be donated to local agencies. There you will also find a form you can fill out to create your own charity for this cause. Below is a list of charities accepting donations at this time and a brief description of each.

Charity Link Description
Action Against Hunger Action Against Hunger is a global humanitarian charity that helps to uproot the cause and help those affected by hunger.
World Vision World Vision is a charity where you can be a sponsor to children in need.
No Kid Hungry No Kid Hungry is a charity that helps provide food to hungry children in the USA. They are also taking measures to help those in need of aid due to the pandemic.
Unicefusa UNICEFUSA is a charity that helps children all over the world. UNICEF does not only protect children from hunger, but it also helps them stay safe during the ongoing pandemic.
The Hunger Project The Hunger Project is a charity to make an impact across Africa, South Asia, and Latin America in regards to alleviating their hunger issues.
